To my dear, Yanie

  I have written this letter to celebrate our one year and two months of our journey together - even the bad, good, arguments and more things we have experienced. I find myself feeling so overwhelmed with gratitude for you, for us and to every moment, smile and challenges we have shared has put us even more together and I cherish this more than words can express.

  But, I want to take this certain moment to address something that’s been weighing heavily on my heart. In recent weeks or even months that I have known I’ve let my emotions get the better of me. I’ve been distant when I should’ve been present, cold when I should’ve been warm, and I’ve let frustration and anger escape in ways that hurt you and for that, I am deeply, deeply sorry.

  Through all of that, your patience and love have been and always been my guiding light. You remind me my bestest part of who I was once before and inspire me to grow and to be focused on my school work and encourage me to try and achieve the things I love. I can’t thank you enough for being there for me, even times where I was invalidating you, pretending to be a victim and more. You were and always have been my greatest gift that comes in my life. I promise to show you every day to move forward with me.

  So cheers to us for our love, our laughter, and our lessons. Let us have the brightest days ahead and for our beautiful future is to build memories with you.


With all my Love,

Gelott
